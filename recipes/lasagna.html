Lasagna

An easy Lasagna recipe that saves me lots of time

This recipe is simple and satisfies every in the family.try it

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 (32 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 32 onunce cottage cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • salt to taste
  • ground black prepper to taste
  • 9 Lasagna needles
  • 2cup water

steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cook ground beef until browned; add spaghetti sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Mix cottage cheese, mozzarella, eggs, Parmesan, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  4. Layer sauce, uncooked noodles, cheese mixture, and sauce in a 9x13-inch dish. Repeat layers, top with cheese, and pour water along edges. Cover with foil.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes, uncover and bake for 10 more minutes. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
