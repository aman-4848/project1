carbonara

An easy carbonara recipe that saves me lots of time

This recipe is simple and satisfies every in the family.try it

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces bacon, diced
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 (12 ounce) jar Alfredo sauce
  • Â½ cup water
  • 3 roma (plum) tomatoes - halved, seeded, and chopped

steps

    1. Cook bacon until browned, about 10 minutes. Drain, leaving 2 tbsp drippings in the skillet.
    2. Cook onion with bacon until translucent, 5-10 minutes. Add garlic and cook for 2 more minutes.
    3. Add Alfredo sauce, water, and scrape the skillet. Simmer, add tomatoes, and cook for 5 minutes.
