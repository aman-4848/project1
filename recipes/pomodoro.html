Pomodoro

An easy pomodoro recipe that saves me lots of time

This recipe is simple and satisfies every in the family.try it

Ingredients

  • Cook bacon until browned, about 10 minutes. Drain, leaving 2 tbsp drippings in the skillet.
  • Cook onion with bacon until translucent, 5-10 minutes. Add garlic and cook for 2 more minutes.
  • Add Alfredo sauce, water, and scrape the skillet. Simmer, add tomatoes, and cook for 5 minutes.
